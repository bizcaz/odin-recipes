EZ grilled cheese sammich

Return to monke

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Some butter
  • A slice of cheese

No, really, that's it.

  1. Butter one (just one) side of each bread slice
  2. Build the sammich in the pan with the butter sides out
  3. Make it crispy on both sides (don't burn it, dummy)
