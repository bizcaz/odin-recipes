Baked Mac and Cheese

Tasty baked mac like grammy made, yo

This shit will blow yer balls off

Ingredients

  • One pound of preferred pasta (elbow works best)
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1 1/2 cup of milk
  • Velveeta cheese
  • Shredded cheddar
  • Any additional cheese you may like (seriously, it doesn't matter; just don't go overboard with the cheeses)
  1. First, cook the pasta so it's ready
  2. Once the pasta is done, melt your butter in a sauce pan
  3. When your butter is melted, add the milk and flour to the pot. Stir the mixture and don't stop until it becomes creamy
  4. After you've mixed your sauce, dump your pasta into a lasanga dish and evenly pour the sauce through the pasta
  5. Add your desired cheeses and place it in the oven at 350Â°F

Tip: You could also try adding the pasta in "layers" and add your sauce and cheese between the layers (sort of like lasanga)
